    ABOUT US

    Motortech Nationwide Corporation (MNC) is the leading multi-brand motorcycle dealer in the Philippines. Its humble beginning can be traced back to the late 1960’s as a motorcycle spare parts and hardware store at a small town in Bicol—Daet, Camarines Norte. Through the dedication, perseverance and hard work of many, the company grew and was incorporated by the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) on December 27, 1979. After more than 4 decades of existence, the company has widened its presence beyond waters as it now operates with more than 500+ branches and satellites across the archipelago. It reaches out to serve in all areas of the country—Luzon, Visayas and Mindanao. In Metro Manila alone, there are more than 100 branches, with more soon to be opened. Expansion is also on the rise across the Visayas and Mindanao.
